# The cap set constant

## Description of constant

$C\_{4}$ is the largest constant such that, for large $n$, there exists subsets of $\mathbb{F}\_{3}^n$ of cardinality $(C\_{4}-o(1))^{n}$ that are *cap sets*, i.e., they do not contain any lines (length three progressions).

## Additional comments and links

- Any cap set $A$ in $\mathbb{F}\_{3}^n$ gives a lower bound $C\_{4} \geq \lvert A\rvert^{1/n}$ by taking Cartesian powers.
- [Wikipedia page on cap sets](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cap_set)
- Also connected to the sunflower conjecture; see [NS2016].
